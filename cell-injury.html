📖 Notes

Definition

Cell injury is the functional and structural change occurring when cells cannot adapt to stress.

Causes

  • Hypoxia
  • Ischemia
  • Chemical injury
  • Physical agents
  • Infections
  • Immune reactions
  • Nutritional disorders
  • Genetic defects

Mechanism

📝 Types of Cell Injury

Reversible Irreversible
• Cell swelling
• Fatty change 		• Membrane damage
• Nuclear changes

⭐ High Yield for NEET PG

  • ATP depletion is the earliest biochemical change.
  • Mitochondria are the first organelles affected.
  • Calcium influx activates destructive enzymes.
  • Reversible injury can recover if the cause is removed.
  • Irreversible injury results in necrosis or apoptosis.
